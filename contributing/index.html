Contributing

Got an idea or suggestion? Use our community forums to share and discuss it. Are you a wiz with troubleshooting? Help answer any posts you run into on the community forums. Ran into an development issue? File a detailed report (version number, what you did, and actual vs expected outcomes) on GitHub. Want uMod support and plugins for a new or favorite game? Hook us up and we'll see what we can do!

Donating

While uMod does have a small team of volunteer developers, the cost of living often takes them away from uMod. A lot of work has gone into developing uMod, and its continued maintenance and support depends on you!
